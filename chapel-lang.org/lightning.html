Lightning Talks BoFs

As part of the annual SC conference series, Chapel has hosted a semi-regular Birds of a Feather (BoF) session featuring Chapel Lightning Talks—very short talks featuring recent work from members of the broad Chapel community. Archives of these talks can be found by following the links below.

  • Chapel Lightning Talks 2014, SC14, New Orleans LA, November 18, 2014.

  • Chapel Lightning Talks 2013, SC13, Denver CO, November 20, 2013.

  • Chapel Lightning Talks 2012, SC12, Salt Lake City UT, November 14, 2012.

  • Chapel Lightning Talks, SC11, Seattle WA, November 16, 2011.
