Ahmad Rezaii

Ahmad is a developer on the Chapel team at HPE. He has worked on Chapel libraries, the compiler, and the build system. He received his M.S. in Computer Science from Boise State University where he developed data processing tools for hydrologists using the ParFlow model. In his free time, Ahmad enjoys mountain biking, whitewater rafting, and spending time with his pets.
